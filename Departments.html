Design Department

This department oversees the creative design, development, and testing of mechanical and robotic equipment.

Electronics Department

This department deals with the circuit, various electronic component and sensors of the robot . They ensures that the robot is working properly and the connections made are correct.

Coding Department

This department deals with the development of website and app for the club and coding part of project. Also, conduct different types of hackathons and coding competitions.

Media Department

This division is responsible for managing the club's all social media platform and creating content for it, such as posters, videos, and other materials.

Documentation Department

This department is responsible for all the official documentation work of the Club.

Research & Development Department

This department is responsible for performing literature reviews and providing the best ideas that will aid in the development of new robot technologies with the club's other departments.
